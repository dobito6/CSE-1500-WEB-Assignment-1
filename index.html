About us

Share Your Journey is a car sharing concept. You've probably seen these red cars with white circles on the street at one time or another. Nowadays there are white electric cars as well. The way it works is simple: just create a Share Your Journey account, download the app and use this to book and unlock a car. Each car has its own fixed designated location where you pick it up and return it after your trip. Maintenance, cleaning, that designated spot, fuel card and more are all included.
